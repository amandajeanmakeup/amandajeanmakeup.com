About

Where technique
meets instinct.

Amanda Jean Prestipino is a DMV-based luxury makeup artist with 8 years of professional experience and formal training from a nationally accredited makeup school in Los Angeles.

Her foundation is technical — rooted in skin theory, face structure, color balance, longevity, and lighting. Not just trends. She brings the precision of a trained professional and the intuition of an artist who has spent thousands of hours in the chair.

"I don't do cookie-cutter. I do you — elevated, effortless, and exactly right."

Amanda's style has a name: Goldilocks Glam. Not too natural. Not too glam. That sweet spot where your makeup enhances everything you already have without replacing a single thing. The looks that make you say "wait" when you see yourself in the mirror.

Every client sits down as themselves and leaves as the best version of themselves. Not someone else's vision. Not whatever is trending. Yours.

She serves brides and clients throughout the DMV, Baltimore, Eastern Shore, and Southern Coastal Delaware — and is available for destination weddings nationwide.
